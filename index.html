About Us

More information about us

KMC School is the junior wing of Kathmandu Model College (KMC), onw of the most reowned colleges of Nepal. It is located at Buddhanagar, 500m South from Nayabaneshwor Chowk. It lies at a peaceful location away from the population overlooking open spaes and greeneries around it, which helps the children to broaden the mental horizon in oen and fresh surrounding. The teachers here have a very friendly and lovely atmosphere towards the children. Here you will find one to one experienced teachers who guide the children step by step. There are many clubs here for extra activities, in which children can develop their interest and hobby in which they want. There are good facilities for children like library, canteen. sports, arts classes and scouts. Here every effort is made to keep the children in discipline and manners. Good teachers and environment have been well taken care of for kindergarten children as well. We guarnteed for the academic excellence of the children. Our school is dedicated to the children's academic environment , proper facilites and take the responsibilties of the childrens .We are still trying to bring lots and lots of good facilities for the kids.
